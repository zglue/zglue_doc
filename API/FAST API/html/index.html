Overview

The glorius ZEUS, mignty of all the Gods, son of a Titan, wielder of lightning, is befuddled by technology of zGlue. A roar! "How in the world is this thing possible!"

That's the power of zglue's FAST technology and this SDK will give you the power to create great objects the envy of gods and goddesses for all time.

As small as the FAST technology is the library holding the key to this power is itself a smidgen of code, minimal calories, lean and mean for the future.

Features highlight:

  • Enable/Disable any or all of the chips with the FAST domain.
  • Power Shedding; turning off power to a chip and prevent even its leakage current.
  • Route internal signals to external pins from you might monitor whatever you need.
  • Changing the power state of the FAST itself.

Supported hardware

Unique to FAST is the support of any hardware available in a wlcsp package though selecting from the glittering collection of validated chips would set you on the path to the Emerald City.
